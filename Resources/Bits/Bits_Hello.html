Bits_Hello

code, output, and build for C++, Rust, and C#

These pages support comparison of fragments of code in several different languages, much as you might compare a sentence of English with one in Spanish to help you learn Spanish. Hello world is the traditional first step with a new language. This page shows how to write, build, and execute that in three languages, C++, Rust, and C#.
