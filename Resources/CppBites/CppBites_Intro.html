CppBites - Introduction

C++ is a large, powerful, and complex language. The pages in this "CppBites" thread discuss the C++ programming language in relatively small bite-sized chunks. It is intended to complement the C++ Story
This thread (sequence of pages) is just beginning. I expect it will grow slowly over the next few months.
The language comes in two parts. The core language was defined by standards C++98 and C++03. Additions, refinements, and simplifications have been added in standards C++11, C++14, C++17, and C++20. The language, as ammended by C++11 ... C++20, is referred to as "modern C++".
