Site Story:

Prologue
States purpose of the site, defines reuse, an enumerates features of the site. Concludes with a summary of potential application domains.
Introduction
Introduces the goals and principal parts of this site, focusing espeically on Repositories and Stories.
Repositories
Discusses conventions for repository contents and the scope of their contents.
Stories
Describes what a story is, and the kinds of stories you will find on this site.
Experiments
An important goal for this site is to experiment and draw conclusions about good ways to document and deploy code. Several experiments trying to improve documentation and deployment are presented, along with conclusions.
Summary
Reminds you what the story told you in brief form.
