9.0Libraries Prologue: The C++ language comes with a great collection of standard libraries. There are scores of very well designed facilities and more are coming with the next standardization in C++20. We will be discussing four of them here - the ones most often used building server and desktop applications. We will also discuss one of the (very many) Boost libraries, Boost::Asio, of which part is very likely to become a networking standard library. I will be working on this chapter intermittently - targetting completion mid Spring 2020.
