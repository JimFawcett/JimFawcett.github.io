    • Bits are short programs that demonstrate HelloWorld, Data, Objects, generics, and iteration for the five languages: C++, Rust, C#, Python, and JavaScript.

    • Stories are ebooks for Rust and C++ with chapters and exercises.
    • Idioms & Patterns contains depricated contents: comparisons of code fragments for C++, Rust, and C#. This material is being replaced with Bits content.
